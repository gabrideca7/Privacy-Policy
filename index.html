Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the Outsyd app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Gabriele Decandia (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is provided "AS IS".

User Provided Information

The Application acquires the information you supply when you download and register the Application.

  • Account Data: When you register, you provide your email address, name, and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication).
  • User Generated Content (UGC): The Application includes social features. If you choose to publish an itinerary, comments, or photos, this content becomes visible to other users of the Application.
  • Media & Camera: The Application requests access to your device's gallery or camera solely to allow you to upload a profile picture. This image is stored securely and displayed on your public profile.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, and information about the way you use the Application.

Location Information

This Application collects your device's location to provide core features:

  • Navigation & Maps: To show your position on the map and provide the "Follow Me" feature during your trips.
  • Itinerary Planning: To suggest starting points for your itineraries based on your current city.

Third Party Access & Services

We transmit data to trusted third-party services to provide the Application's functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics): Used for secure login, database storage, and app stability. Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI: Used to generate intelligent itinerary suggestions. Anonymized prompts are sent to generate travel plans.
  • Geoapify: Used for geocoding (finding coordinates of cities and places).
  • OpenRouteService: Used to calculate travel routes and distances on the map.
  • Formspree: Used only if you submit a feedback form via the settings menu.

Data Retention & Deletion

The Service Provider will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly within the Application's Settings menu ("Delete Account" button). Upon deletion, your personal data is permanently removed from our servers.

Children

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, please contact the Service Provider so that they will be able to take the necessary actions.

Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards (such as HTTPS encryption and secure Firebase infrastructure) to protect information we process and maintain.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.
